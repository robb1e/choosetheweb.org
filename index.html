Helping to spread the web

We use the web all the time we could be in danger of taking it for granted. We thought we should take a timeout to remind ourselves of the why and how's of the web. We have listed some of the canonical sources to help us, and hopefully others, appreciate the virtues of and dangers to the web as we know it.

Reading list

Long live the web Tim Berners-Lee

The 20th anniversary of the web allowed Berners-Lee to reflect on the progress the web has made in it's 20 years. Berners-Lee stresses what the web has enabled as well as what the challenges are to the universal principles that are being threatened by current trends.

Cool URIs don't change Tim Berners-Lee

A nice explanation of why URIs often get changed, and why that should be avoided.
